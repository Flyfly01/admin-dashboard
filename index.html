Crate training

From day 1, you are going to start crate training. This will help with anxiety and give you a comfortable place to call your own. We will make it a comforting positive place for you

Potty training

You are coming home at a rather early age of 6 weeks as opposed the normal 8 or 12 weeks. We are prepared for accidents as you don't have full control over your bladder yet. But we will work on it diligently
